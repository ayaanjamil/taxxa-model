Foreign specialists qualifying for key-personnel status under §13 EPL are subject to a flat source-tax withholding rate of 32% on earned income, replacing the progressive schedule that would otherwise apply.

The tax card is valid for a maximum of 48 months from the start of Finnish employment §14a EPL. This was extended by the 2022 reform from the prior 36-month limit; the earlier version §14 EPL (pre-2022) is now superseded. Reapplication rules were also clarified at that time.

To qualify, the specialist must receive a salary exceeding €5,800/month §13.1 EPL and hold a technical, managerial, or expert role not ordinarily available in the Finnish labour market. The Tax Administration bulletin VH/2024/00156 details the employer documentation required.

Applications must be filed within 90 days of the first working day §15 EPL. Late submissions are rejected, as confirmed in KHO 2021:88.
